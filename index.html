UC 00
synchronous execution
One Instant. Zero Failure Points.
Today, a transaction touching Ethereum L1 and two L2s requires three sequential confirmations — each chain waits for the previous one to finalise. EEZ uses ZK proofs to pre-verify all state transitions, then executes across every chain simultaneously in a single Ethereum block. All nodes execute, verify, and settle together — or the whole batch reverts.

bridging eliminated
Verify → Flow → Settle. Atomic.
Traditional bridges introduce three failure points: the source lock, the relay, and the destination mint. Any one can fail, leaving users stranded. Bridge hacks are the single largest source of DeFi losses by dollar value. EEZ replaces all three steps with one atomic Ethereum-settled transaction — no trusted operator, no relay delay, no slippage between chains.

One Transaction. One Atomic Flow.
Complex DeFi strategies across chains today require 4–6 separate transactions: bridge to L2, approve token, deposit collateral, borrow, swap, bridge back. Verify Once. Access Everywhere.
KYC and identity credentials are chain-specific today. A wallet verified on Ethereum must re-verify on every L2 it wants to use — separately for DEX access, payment rails, and trading venues.
